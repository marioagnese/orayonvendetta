What is AI Arbitrage?

AI Arbitrage is the simple business of helping small companies use existing AI tools to save time and money — without building any new AI yourself.

You act as the middle person: you find businesses that are still doing things manually, install ready-made AI agents for them (chatbots, lead systems, content automation, etc.), and charge a setup fee plus optional monthly management.
